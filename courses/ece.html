**Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)** is a branch of engineering that focuses on designing, developing, and testing electronic circuits, communication systems, and related technologies. It bridges core electronics with modern communication systems like satellite, wireless, and optical communication.

### ðŸŒŸ **Core Subjects in ECE:**

1. **Electronic Devices and Circuits (EDC):** Semiconductors, diodes, transistors, etc.

2. **Digital Electronics:** Logic gates, flip-flops, counters, and microprocessors.

3. **Analog Circuits:** Operational amplifiers (op-amps), filters, and oscillators.

4. **Communication Systems:** AM/FM, modulation, demodulation, and multiplexing.

5. **Microprocessors and Microcontrollers:** 8085, 8051 microcontrollers, embedded systems.

6. **Signal and Systems:** Fourier transforms, Laplace transforms, and convolution.

7. **VLSI Design:** Designing integrated circuits (ICs) using hardware description languages (HDL).

8. **Electromagnetic Theory:** Antennas, wave propagation, and transmission lines.
